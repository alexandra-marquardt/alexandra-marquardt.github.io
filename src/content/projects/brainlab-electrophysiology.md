As an R&D Software Engineer at Brainlab AG, I developed an augmented reality application for electrophysiological interventions that visualized cardiac muscle segmentations and conductivity mappings as an interactive 3D model. The system was designed to support intraoperative orientation during ablation procedures by making relevant anatomy and signal information easier to inspect in space.


## Highlights
- Built a Magic Leap 2 application that combines cardiac segmentations and electrophysiological mappings in a shared 3D view.
- Implemented interactive visualization controls so clinicians can inspect and reposition the model during the workflow.
- Worked with clinicians from Augsburg Hospital to support clinical validation sessions.
