At the Kamitani Lab for AI in Neuroscience at Kyoto University, I studied how reliably deep learning-based reconstruction pipelines recover color information from brain activity. The project focused on diagnosing failure modes in existing methods and testing targeted modifications that improve color fidelity while preserving overall reconstruction quality.

## Highlights

- Evaluated how accurately brain activity-based reconstruction pipelines preserve stimulus color information.
- Identified key weaknesses in the baseline approach and traced where color information was lost in the reconstruction process.
- Implemented and tested pipeline modifications that improved color fidelity, while exposing the trade-off with overall reconstruction quality.
