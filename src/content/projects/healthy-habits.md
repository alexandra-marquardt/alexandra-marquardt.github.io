As part of the TUM:Junge Akademie research scholarship, I translated health science findings into a mobile app that encourages users to build healthier routines through small, personalized daily tasks. The product combined behavioral guidance with social features so participants could share progress, stay accountable, and support each other during beta testing.


## Highlights
- Designed and built a social app with real-time synchronization for users, tasks, posts, and activity feeds.
- Worked with health scientists to define daily interventions grounded in behavior and lifestyle research.
- Developed the backend data model for accounts, personalized tasks, and shared progress updates.
- Released the app through the app stores for a beta testing phase.
