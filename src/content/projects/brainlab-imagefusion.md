As an R&D Software Engineer at Brainlab AG, I built the first deep learning pipeline for real-time fusion of preoperative and intraoperative imaging in deforming organs such as the lung and liver. The goal was to create a technical foundation for navigation workflows that combine the anatomical context of CT with the live feedback of ultrasound.


## Highlights
- Built the initial training and inference pipeline for real-time fusion of preoperative CT and intraoperative ultrasound.
- Structured the workflow to support multimodal alignment in anatomies affected by motion and deformation.
- Ran first proof-of-concept experiments on an initial dataset to validate the setup.
