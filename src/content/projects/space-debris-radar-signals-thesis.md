Bachelor’s Thesis at the lab of Prof. Stephan Günnemann (TUM), in collaboration with Actlabs, focused on deep learning-based space debris detection in Low Earth Orbit using physics-inspired radar simulation.

## Highlights

- Built a deep learning pipeline for space debris detection from simulated radar returns.
- Generated synthetic radar I/Q sequences from radar cross-section models of satellite-mounted sensors.
- Developed a Matlab-based simulation pipeline for 94 GHz monostatic space-borne radar.
- Evaluated LSTM, ResNet, and Transformer models, achieving ~95% classification accuracy.
- Conducted robustness tests under occlusion, clutter, and sensor saturation.
