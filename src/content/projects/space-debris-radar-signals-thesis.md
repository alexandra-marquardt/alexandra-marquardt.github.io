Bachelor thesis project on deep learning-based space debris detection in Low Earth Orbit using physics-inspired radar simulations and synthetic I/Q echo sequences.

## Highlights

- Built a Matlab-based simulation pipeline for 94 GHz monostatic space-borne radar.
- Generated synthetic radar I/Q sequences from RCS signatures of multiple debris shapes.
- Evaluated LSTM, ResNet, and Transformer models, achieving ~95% classification accuracy.
- Conducted robustness tests under occlusion, clutter, and sensor saturation.
